About Us

CITS is a web solution company, which was founded in 2007 by Sulaiman saeed under ennovate Nigeria limited, and later branched out to be incoporated as a company under CAC in 2011 as CITS limited.

in 2010, he teamed up with Kabeer a web programmer and online customer service specialist at the time, to handle the responsibilities to consolidate the business as a partner and co-founder. kabeer has managed the reins of CITS as the chief Executive office, building the company to where it currently is today.

over time, CITS has grown to be one of the top three web hostning companies in Nigeria, providing service for clients inside and outside Nigeria.

Focused on delivering customer support, CITS prides itself on delivering quality assistance and delivering a seamless approach to helping every customer throught phone. calls, chart, email, and support tickedt.

CITS is accredited google Apps Reseller, NG registrar, cloudfare certified partner as well as interswi partner for payment intergration solution.
